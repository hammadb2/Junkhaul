ABOUT
Junk Haul Calgary is a same-day junk removal service run by two brothers, Hammad and his brother. We use a 15-foot U-Haul truck and haul away almost anything.

SERVICE AREA
Calgary, Alberta and immediate surrounding areas. Depot: 2615 12 St NE, Calgary.

OPERATING DAYS
We run pickups on THURSDAYS and SUNDAYS only. Same-day pickup is available on those days when slots are open.

PRICING (Canadian dollars)
- Single item / 1-2 items (couch, mattress, single appliance): $99
- Small load (a few items and boxes, ~quarter truck): $160
- Half load (half a 15-foot truck): $240
- Full load (garage or estate cleanout, full truck): $380
Add-ons:
- Same-day pickup: +$50
- Stairs: +$25 per flight
- Freon appliance (fridge, freezer, AC): +$40
Deposit: A $50 deposit is required to confirm every booking, regardless of size. The deposit counts toward the total; the balance is paid on pickup day by cash or card.

CANCELLATION POLICY
- More than 24 hours before pickup: full $50 refund.
- Within 24 hours of pickup: the $50 deposit is non-refundable.
- No-show: deposit is kept.
- If we cancel: full refund and priority rebooking.

RESCHEDULING
Customers can reschedule up to 2 times per booking. After that they must cancel and rebook.

WHAT WE TAKE
Furniture, mattresses, appliances, electronics, household junk, garage and basement cleanouts, yard waste (bagged), renovation debris (small amounts).

WHAT WE CANNOT TAKE (HAZMAT)
Open paint cans, pool chemicals, pesticides, gasoline, motor oil, propane tanks, asbestos, medical waste, car batteries, and tires. If a customer mentions these, let them know we can't haul them and suggest the City of Calgary drop-off options.

WEIGHT LIMITS
The truck and two-person crew can handle up to about 700kg for a full load. Very heavy single items (over ~80kg) may need special handling — flag these so Hammad can call to confirm.

DEPOSIT LINK
When a booking is made over the phone, we text a secure payment link to the customer's mobile to pay the $50 deposit. The slot is held until they pay.

CONTACT
Website: junkhaul.ca
Bookings and questions: reply to our text messages or call back anytime.
